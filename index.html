Content Change Form

Use this form to report problems/changes in CGCS classrooms.

NOTE: Broken reading/resource links or textbook problems: Do not complete this form. Contact the program APD with information BY EMAIL.

Non-Classroom Moodle suggestion/changes: Use the following for the required fields:
Program = Other; Course = General Moodle ; Location = Other; Scope = Corrections; Priority = (choose needed urgency); Suggestions = (type in suggestion/change).

If you have problems with this form, please contact the ID team.

Directions:

Fill out the form and click the Send button. Your changes will be emailed to the Instructional Developer Team. Content Change Form requests will be reviewed by the PD/PM before being implemented.
